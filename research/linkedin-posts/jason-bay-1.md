# Jason Bay - Offer-Based Cold Outreach Strategy

## Source
LinkedIn Post (Jason Bay - Outbound Squad CEO)

## Key Insight
Cold outreach effectiveness is no longer driven by messaging alone, but by the value of the "offer" presented to the prospect.

Offers increase email reply rates by up to 28% (Gong research).

---

## Core Framework

### 1. Pitch The Blind Date
Introduce a relevant expert or internal stakeholder as the meeting value.

- Focus: “Who you will meet”
- Example: Sales engineer or domain expert intro

---

### 2. 1:Many Offers
Scalable insights that apply to multiple prospects.

- Industry benchmarks
- Competitive analysis
- Best practice reports

---

### 3. 1:1 Offers
Highly personalized value tailored to a specific company.

- Custom audits
- Performance benchmarking
- Personalized reports
- On-site analysis or evaluation

---

## Strategic Takeaway
Modern outbound is shifting from “asking for time” to “offering value upfront.”

The key question in outreach is no longer:
"Can we schedule a meeting?"

But:
"What are we giving the buyer that makes the meeting worth their time even if they never buy?"
