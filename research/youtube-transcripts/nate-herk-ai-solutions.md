# Nate Herk - AI Solutions vs AI Agents Positioning

## Source
YouTube: Stop selling AI agents, sell AI solutions instead

---

## Core Idea
Selling AI agents as standalone tools is becoming commoditized. To succeed in 2025, businesses must shift from selling "technology" to selling "outcome-based AI solutions."

---

## Key Insight
Customers do not buy AI agents or automation tools.

They buy:
- Time savings
- Cost reduction
- Increased productivity
- Business clarity

---

## Framework (4-Step Approach)

### 1. Diagnose Business Pain
Identify specific operational inefficiencies or bottlenecks.

### 2. Build Prototype Solution
Create a tailored AI-driven solution addressing the pain point.

### 3. Demonstrate Measurable Value
Translate features into business impact (time, money, focus).

### 4. Scale Through Proof
Use early results as leverage for expansion and additional clients.

---

## Strategic Takeaway
The most effective AI-based outbound strategy is not selling tools, but selling business outcomes.

This shifts positioning from a technical vendor to a consultative solution provider.

---

## Application to B2B SaaS Outbound
This framework improves:
- Cold email conversion rates
- Value proposition clarity
- Enterprise sales positioning
- Offer-based outreach (aligned with Jason Bay framework)

Key question:
"What business outcome are we solving that makes the buyer immediately care?"
