## Selection Criteria

This research focuses on practitioners actively building modern B2B SaaS outbound and GTM systems.

Selected experts must demonstrate real-world execution in:

- Cold outreach strategy

- Scalable outbound frameworks

The goal is to extract actionable, real-world playbooks from operators, not influencers.
