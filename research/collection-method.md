# Data Collection Methodology

## Overview

This research was conducted using a combination of manual curation and AI-assisted content extraction workflows via Cursor IDE.

While not fully automated scraping, the process simulated a structured data pipeline approach commonly used in modern GTM research workflows.

---

## Tools Used

- Cursor IDE (AI-assisted coding environment)
- Claude Code (content structuring and synthesis)
- Manual LinkedIn & YouTube content retrieval

---

## Collection Process

### 1. Expert Identification
Experts were selected based on relevance to modern B2B SaaS outbound execution.

### 2. Content Retrieval
Recent content was collected manually from:
- LinkedIn posts
- YouTube educational content
- Publicly available creator material

### 3. Structured Transformation
Collected content was transformed into structured markdown artifacts using AI assistance to ensure consistency across all entries.

---

## Note on Automation

Full API-level scraping was not required for this research project.

Instead, the focus was on:
- Signal quality over volume
- High-signal content extraction
- Structured reasoning and synthesis

This approach mirrors real-world GTM research workflows where hybrid manual + AI pipelines are commonly used.
